One Nito account reaches many models from several providers. You do not sign up with each provider or hold their keys. You name a model by its ID in your request, and you choose how private that call is with an optional suffix on the same ID. The catalog is discovered live from providers, so `GET /v1/models` is always the source of truth for what you can call.
