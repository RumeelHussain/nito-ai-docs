How Nito treats your data splits into two questions: what is visible while a request is being served, and what survives once it is done. The default is **metadata only** — request and response bodies are request-local and are not persisted.

## In this section

- [**Data visibility**](/privacy/data-visibility) — who and what sees a request while it is in flight.
- [**Data retention**](/privacy/data-retention/index) — what survives a request and for how long, and the metadata-only default.
- [**Metadata-only default**](/privacy/data-retention/metadata-only-default) — the baseline: bodies are request-local, responses are `Cache-Control: no-store`, and telemetry is restricted to a safe allowlist.
