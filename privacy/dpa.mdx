A Data Processing Addendum (DPA) is the contract that sets out how a service provider handles data on your behalf: the roles of each party, the categories of data processed, the security commitments, the subprocessors involved, and the process for executing and amending the agreement. This page is the home for Nito's DPA. The DPA text and its execution process are not present in the codebase these docs are built from, so this page does not reproduce or paraphrase terms. It marks what is coming and who owns it.

<Warning>
No DPA text is published here yet. Do not treat anything on this page as contractual. The agreement and its terms are authored and approved by Legal before publication.
</Warning>
