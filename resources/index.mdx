The Resources tab collects the material you reach for once you are past the basics: guides for building well, integrations for shipping telemetry, and the trust and compliance references your team may need. It is not tutorial material — for that, start with [How Nito Works](/get-started/how-nito-works).

Core reference tables live with the topics they belong to: **[Pricing](/models/pricing)** sits with Models, and **[errors & rate limits](/api-reference/errors-and-rate-limits/error-codes)** live in the API reference.

## What's Here

- [**Best practices**](/resources/best-practices/latency-and-performance) — guides for latency and performance, cost control, secure API-key handling, privacy-safe logging, uptime and fallbacks, and a production-readiness checklist.
- [**Observability**](/resources/observability/index) — ship logs, traces, and metrics to Datadog, Langfuse, Sentry, or S3, or receive them over your own webhooks.
- [**Trust Center**](/resources/trust-center/index) — DPA, subprocessors, SLA, security advisories, and responsible disclosure.
