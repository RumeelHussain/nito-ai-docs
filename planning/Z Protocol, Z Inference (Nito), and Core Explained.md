# **Core, Z, and Nito: Positioning and Master Narrative**

**Status:** Internal briefing doc. Use to align team members, advisors, and the PR firm on how to describe the three entities.

**Important:** Nito is not yet publicly announced. It is presently known as **Z Inference**. 

---

## **Master Narrative**

Three distinct surfaces, each with its own story:

* **Core** is the Bitcoin Power Grid. The infrastructure platform for Bitcoin products and other technologies built on Satoshi Plus consensus. Bitcoin yield, Bitcoin collateral, Bitcoin-based products.

* **Z** is a private AI platform. It gives humans and agents private inference, private execution, and reliable agent workloads on one substrate.

* **Nito** is the killer app on Z. A private multi-LLM interface for humans and agents. Today it lives under the name *Z Inference* at the protocol layer; Nito is the consumer brand and the planned rebrand.

Each brand stays in its own lane. Core keeps a clean Bitcoin narrative. Z leads with private AI. Nito leads with product utility. Z and Nito will have overlap with each other; Core does not overlap with either, though Core’s surface will occasionally reference Z as the first Satoshi Plus sister chain.

## **Core: The Bitcoin Power Grid**

**Positioning.** Core is the infrastructure platform for Bitcoin products and other technologies built on Satoshi Plus consensus. Bitcoin has matured into a serious asset (institutions are the most visible adopters, but the surface is broader than institutions alone), and Core is the platform built around that reality: Bitcoin yield, Bitcoin collateral, Bitcoin-based products. Core’s consensus can also power sister chains, with Z as the first.

**What Core does:** \- Powers Bitcoin yield, Bitcoin collateral, and Bitcoin-based products. \- Anchors Bitcoin-focused DeFi (lending, liquidity, structured products). \- Can power sister chains via Satoshi Plus, extending the consensus model to new ecosystems. Z is the first. \- Maintains a clean, focused Bitcoin narrative.

**Z connection on Core’s surface.** Z is the first Satoshi Plus sister chain and licenses Core’s technology. That relationship adds value to Core. Don’t get specific about the economic mechanics on Core’s public surface.

---

## **Z: A Private AI Platform**

**Positioning.** Z is a private AI platform built around private inference and private execution. It gives humans and autonomous agents everything they can do with AI today, now private, with reliable execution for agent workloads on the same substrate. The go-to-market is product-led.

**Primary tagline.** *Everything you can do with AI. Now private.* **Sub-line.** *Private Inference. Private Agents.*

**What Z does:** \- Provides a private AI platform for humans and autonomous agents. \- Powers private execution through the Z Utilities (Z Trade, Z Lend, USDZ, Z Stake, Z Bridge). \- Hosts the Z token economy. \- Gives agents the reliable, verifiable execution surface they need to run high-value workloads.

**How privacy works on Z.** Privacy is selectable. Workloads pick the tier they need: anonymized routing for lightweight calls, TEE-sealed execution for high-assurance workloads. On-chain action runs through the ShieldedPool with ZK proofs binding the round-trip.

**Why Z, why now.** Frontier labs stop at the model. Agents need more: private inference, reliable execution, and a substrate where they can act. Z is the platform that delivers all three end to end.

## **Nito: Z’s Killer App**

**Positioning.** Nito is the killer app on Z. A private multi-LLM interface for humans and agents. Today the same product is called *Z Inference* at the protocol layer; Nito is the consumer brand and the planned rebrand. Same engine, same gateway, same privacy guarantees. Different surface for a different audience.

**Tagline.** Nito shares Z’s tagline: *Everything you can do with AI. Now private.* Nito is the private inference half of *Private Inference. Private Agents.* today; over time it will also be the starting point for private agents.

**What Nito does:** \- One key, many models. Call any major model through a single API. \- Selectable privacy per call. Workloads pick the right tier, from anonymized frontier-model routing to privately served open-source models to TEE-sealed inference at the top tier. \- Uncensored model access. Reach models without the filtering layer the major labs impose. (Especially relevant in light of recent platform-side model changes elsewhere in the market.) \- Subscription consolidation. One bill replaces multiple subscriptions across the major model providers. \- Zero-knowledge team and collaboration features. Shared usage, shared keys, shared model access with full privacy guarantees throughout. \- Powered by Z. The privacy and execution guarantees come from Z underneath.

**Why Nito.** Nito is Z’s Web2 brand. It’s the product rather than the blockchain, and that separation matters for the audiences that don’t want to think about crypto, especially businesses and enterprises. Nito is still accessible via wallets and can be paid for with tokens; it just doesn’t lead with that surface. Web2 buyers get private inference as a product. Crypto-fluent users still get the full Z surface underneath.

**Voice and surface.** Clean SaaS. Linear / Vercel / Stripe energy. No crypto jargon. No mention of ZEC, validators, or the Z token in Nito’s day-to-day surface. The only Z reference is a “Powered by Z” mark.

## **Messaging Focus by Account**

### **Core’s surface**

* Lead with the Bitcoin Power Grid: Bitcoin yield, Bitcoin collateral, Bitcoin-based products.

* Position Core as the infrastructure platform that can power sister chains, with Z as the first.

* Reference Z when it strengthens the story (first Satoshi Plus sister chain licensing Core’s tech). Don’t get specific about the economic mechanics.

* Protected audience: CORE holders. Never make them feel abandoned; Z extends Satoshi Plus into private AI without taking anything from Core.

### **Z’s surface**

* Lead with the AI-first narrative. *Everything you can do with AI. Now private.*

* Privacy is the wedge. Reliable agent workloads are the proof point.

* Refer to the inference product as **Z Inference** in crypto-native contexts.

* Reference Core only when it strengthens the story. Canonical line: *Z licenses Core’s technology as the first Satoshi Plus sister chain.* Past-tense team-experience references are fine (“founding contributor to Core”).

### **Nito’s surface (internal until announced)**

* Refer to the product as **Nito**.

* Position against OpenRouter and the major foundation-model APIs.

* Lead with product utility: multi-model access, selectable privacy, uncensored model access, subscription consolidation, zero-knowledge team features.

* “Powered by Z” mark is the only Z reference.

* A Nito user discovering they were using Z is a feature. Pushing them into the token story before they are ready is a leak.

## **Common Misframings to Correct**

| Wrong | Right |
| :---- | :---- |
| “Z is a Core fork.” | Z is an independent protocol that licenses Core’s technology as the first Satoshi Plus sister chain. |
| “Z is incubated by Core.” | Several Z co-founders came from Core. Z licenses Core’s technology. The protocols are independent. |
| “Z is an app chain / L1 / L2 / sidechain.” | Z is a protocol or a private AI platform. Avoid “chain” descriptors. |
| “Z Inference and Nito are different products.” | Same product. Nito is the planned consumer rebrand of Z Inference. |
| “Nito is a sub-product of Z.” | Nito is Z’s killer app and its own brand. Cross-references are discoverable, not loud. |
| “All Nito inference runs in TEEs.” | TEE-sealed execution is the highest privacy tier. Nito’s privacy is selectable across tiers. |
| “Nito uses ZK proofs to verify inference.” | Inference verifiability comes from TEE attestation. ZK secures on-chain action through the ShieldedPool. |
| “Core is just for institutions.” | Bitcoin’s most visible adopters are institutions, but Core is an infrastructure platform for Bitcoin products and other technologies. The audience is broader. |
